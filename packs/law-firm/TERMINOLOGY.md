# Law firm terminology

| Term | Operational meaning |
|---|---|
| Prospective client | Person or organization whose intake is confidential before engagement |
| Matter | Permission boundary for records, people, time, and work |
| Conflict candidate | Possible relationship match requiring human disposition |
| Privileged | A recorded legal classification, never a model assumption |
| Draft | Work product that cannot be relied on or sent until designated lawyer review |
