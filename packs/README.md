# Industry packs

Packs specialize the universal engine without modifying it. A pack supplies terminology, stricter governance, relevant skills, integration recipes, and safe seed-memory structure; company onboarding adds the client's actual details.

The reference packs cover a marketing agency, a law firm, and a healthcare practice at different depths. Other engagements may cover construction, e-commerce, real estate, accounting, insurance, logistics, recruiting, hospitality, nonprofits, professional services, education, manufacturing, home services, or another field.

**Your industry not here? That is the point:** the engine is reusable, and the pack is built around how each client actually operates.
