# Meridian Creative

Meridian Creative is a 12-person marketing agency based in Providence, Rhode Island. The agency serves B2B software and professional-services companies through positioning, campaign strategy, content, paid media, and reporting.

The operating problem is ordinary and expensive: leads occasionally sit untouched, project status is scattered across meetings and tools, reports consume senior time, and client preferences live in individual memory. Meridian OS coordinates that work while leaving HubSpot, Notion, Slack, Gmail, Calendar, and Stripe as systems of record.
