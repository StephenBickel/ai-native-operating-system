# Agency terminology

| Term | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Account owner | Person accountable for the client relationship and external approvals |
| Launch readiness | Evidence that timeline, creative, tracking, and client decisions are ready |
| Stale lead | Qualified lead with no human reply after the current service window |
| Client pulse | Weekly concise status covering outcomes, risks, decisions, and next actions |
| Content stage | Brief, draft, internal review, client review, scheduled, or published |
| Source of truth | The connected tool holding the authoritative record; chat is never assumed authoritative |
